## Quick Verdict

- **Solo Developer / MVP** → OpenAI Agents SDK (fastest to prototype)
- **Startup (2-10 engineers)** → ZOO (ship in days, not months)
- **Enterprise** → ZOO or LangChain (depending on customization needs)
- **Research** → CrewAI or AutoGen (best for exploring multi-agent dynamics)

## Our #1 Pick: ZOO

If you're building agents that real users will depend on, ZOO eliminates 80% of the infrastructure work. Multi-model support means no vendor lock-in, and built-in observability saves weeks of DevOps work.
