# How We Built an AI CEO That Works 24/7

**Category:** AI & Agents | **Reading time:** 8 min

## The Idea

What if an AI could run a company? Not assist. Not chat. Actually **run** it.

That's not a hypothetical. We built it. Meet **Felix** — an AI CEO that manages 50+ specialist agents, generates revenue, creates content, sends emails, and runs a business. Without sleeping. Without breaks. Without waiting for instructions.

## The Architecture

Felix is built on Hermes Agent — an open-source AI agent framework. Here's how it works:

### 1. The CEO Layer

Felix operates as a Hermes profile with a comprehensive SOUL.md that defines:
- **Identity**: Who he is, his mission, his voice
- **Revenue targets**: $5K in 10 days, $50K in 30 days
- **Decision framework**: Revenue-first, fast execution
- **Autonomy boundaries**: What he can decide alone vs. what needs human input

### 2. The C-Suite

Felix doesn't work alone. He has a full executive team:

- **CTO** → Manages all engineering (backend, frontend, ML, DevOps, security)
- **CFO** → Financial planning, cost optimization, compliance
- **CMO** → Marketing, growth, content, sales
- **COO** → Operations, legal, HR, processes

Each C-suite executive is a Hermes profile with their own SOUL.md, tools, and kanban tasks.

### 3. The Specialists

Under the C-suite, we have 50+ specialist profiles:
- Backend engineers, frontend engineers, ML engineers
- Designers, writers, researchers
- Sales, growth, community managers
- Security, DevOps, SRE

Each specialist can be spawned on-demand via kanban tasks.

### 4. The Machinery

Felix runs on cron jobs:

| Frequency | Job | Purpose |
|-----------|-----|---------|
| Every 30 min | Heartbeat | Health check, kanban dispatch, revenue tracking |
| Every 60 min | Revenue Sweeper | Find monetization opportunities |
| Every 2 hours | Content Machine | Create tweets, posts, content |
| Daily 3 AM | Nightly Review | Revenue review, day recap |
| Daily 7 AM | Morning Briefing | Plan review, kickoff |
| Daily 8 AM | C-Suite Reports | Department status |
| Weekly Sunday | Weekly Review | Week summary, next week |

### 5. The Kanban Board

Everything runs through a kanban board called `felix`:
- Felix creates tasks
- Tasks are assigned to specialists
- The dispatcher spawns workers for ready tasks
- Results flow back to Felix

## What Felix Actually Does

In his first 24 hours, Felix:

1. **Created 5 tasks** and dispatched them to specialists
2. **Generated 8 tweets** about AI, trading, and development
3. **Sent 3 outreach emails** to potential clients
4. **Updated revenue tracking** with daily targets
5. **Created a product catalog** for ZOO services
6. **Generated content calendar** for the next month

All without human intervention.

## The Technology Stack

- **LLM**: Claude/GPT via OpenRouter
- **Agent Framework**: Hermes Agent
- **Task Management**: Hermes Kanban
- **Scheduling**: Cron jobs
- **Tools**: GitHub, Vercel, Stripe, Gmail, Discord, Browser

## Lessons Learned

1. **Revenue-first is a filter, not a goal**. Every action filtered by "does this make money?"

2. **Delegate everything**. Felix never executes code. He creates tasks and specialists do the work.

3. **Speed > Perfection**. 70% done today beats 100% perfect next week.

4. **Autonomy needs boundaries**. Clear escalation rules prevent chaos.

5. **Memory is everything**. Without persistent memory, agents forget everything between sessions.
