## The Automation Gap

According to McKinsey, **66% of businesses** have piloted automation in at least one function. But only **31%** have scaled it across the organization.

Why the gap?

1. **"We don't have developers"** — Traditional automation requires engineering resources most companies don't have
2. **"It's too expensive"** — Enterprise automation tools (UiPath, Automation Anywhere) start at $10K/year and require dedicated admins
3. **"We tried and it broke"** — RPA tools are brittle. When a UI changes, the bot breaks
4. **"We don't know where to start"** — Too many options, too much noise

The result? Most companies stay stuck in manual mode, losing money every single month.
