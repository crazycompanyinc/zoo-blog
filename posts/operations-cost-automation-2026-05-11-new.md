## Why Most "Automation" Doesn't Work

You've probably tried:
- **Zapier/Make** — Great for simple flows, breaks at complexity
- **Offshore VAs** — Cheap but inconsistent, high management overhead
- **RPA tools** — Fragile, breaks when UI changes
- **Hiring more people** — Expensive, slow, doesn't scale

The problem isn't that automation doesn't work. It's that **most tools automate tasks, not processes.**

A task is "send an email." A process is "when a deal moves to 'negotiation,' send a personalized follow-up, update the CRM, notify the account manager, and schedule a check-in call."

That's what AI agents do differently.
