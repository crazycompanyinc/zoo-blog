❓ FAQ

Can I use Vercel and Railway together?

Yes! Many teams use Vercel for their frontend and Railway for their backend APIs and databases. This gives you the best of both worlds.

Is DigitalOcean good for beginners?

DigitalOcean has a steeper learning curve than Vercel or Railway, but their documentation is excellent. If you're comfortable with Linux and basic networking, it's very approachable.

Which platform scales best?

Vercel and DigitalOcean both scale excellently. Vercel handles scaling automatically, while DigitalOcean gives you more control. Railway is best for small-to-medium workloads.

What about AWS, GCP, or Azure?

The cloud giants are powerful but complex. For most startups, Vercel, Railway, or DigitalOcean will be faster to deploy and easier to manage. Consider AWS/GCP when you need specific enterprise services.
