**Time wasted:** 45 min/day per employee
**Annual cost (10-person team):** $97,500

Someone is manually copying customer data from emails into a CRM. Someone else is reformatting spreadsheets for the weekly report. Another person is updating inventory counts by hand.

This isn't a job. It's a bug in your process.

### 2. Report Generation
**Time wasted:** 30 min/day per employee
**Annual cost (10-person team):** $65,000

Every Monday, someone spends 2-3 hours pulling data from 4 different tools, formatting it into a slide deck, and emailing it to leadership.

An AI agent can do this in 4 minutes. Every Monday. Without being asked.

### 3. Email Triage & Response
**Time wasted:** 40 min/day per employee
**Annual cost (10-person team):$86,700

Your team reads the same types of emails every day. "Where's my invoice?" "Can you update my address?" "What's the status of order #4521?"

These aren't complex problems. They're repetitive ones. And they're consuming your most expensive resource: human attention.

### 4. Scheduling & Coordination
**Time wasted:** 25 min/day per employee
**Annual cost (10-person team):** $54,200

Back-and-forth emails to schedule meetings. Checking availability. Rescheduling. Sending reminders.

This was a problem that should have been solved in 2015.

### 5. Quality Checks & Approvals
**Time wasted:** 35 min/day per employee
**Annual cost (10-person team):** $75,800

Manual review of forms, documents, invoices. Checking for errors. Routing to the right person.

Humans are terrible at repetitive quality checks. They get bored. They miss things. They're expensive.
