💡 How to Maximize Your Affiliate Earnings

1. Write Tutorials, Not Reviews

Tutorial content converts 3-5x better than product reviews. Write "How to deploy a Next.js app on Vercel" instead of "Vercel review." The reader is already motivated to sign up.

2. Stack Multiple Programs

A single "Complete AI Startup Stack" post can include 5-8 affiliate links. Each tool serves a different need (hosting, database, email, design), so there's no conflict.

3. Focus on Recurring Commissions

Kit (50% first year + 10-20% recurring) and Supabase (10-20% recurring) are goldmines. One referral can pay you for years. Prioritize programs with recurring revenue.

4. Use Comparison Tables

"Vercel vs Railway vs DigitalOcean" posts capture high-intent search traffic. Developers searching for comparisons are ready to choose — and your affiliate link can be the tiebreaker.
