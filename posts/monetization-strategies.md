# From $0 to $10K MRR: Monetization Strategies That Actually Work

**Category:** Business | **Reading time:** 12 min

## The Dream

You've built something cool. Now you need to pay rent.

Going from $0 to $10K MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) is the hardest jump. Here's how to do it.

## Strategy 1: Sell What You Know (Consulting)

**Fastest path to first dollar.**

If you have expertise, someone will pay for it.

### How to Start
1. Define your niche: "I help X achieve Y"
2. Set your rate: $100-200/hr for specialized work
3. Find clients: Twitter, LinkedIn, referrals
4. Deliver results: Get testimonials
5. Raise rates: Every 3-6 months

### Pros
- Immediate revenue
- No product needed
- Builds relationships

### Cons
- Time = money (no scale)
- Hard to get started
- Client management overhead

### Real Numbers
- 10 clients × $1,500/mo = $15,000/mo
- But you're working 60+ hours/week

---

## Strategy 2: Digital Products (Templates, Tools, Courses)

**Build once, sell forever.**

### Templates & Boilerplates
Package your code. Sell it.

- Next.js SaaS Starter: $29-99
- Design System: $29-49
- EA Templates: $49-199

**Platforms**: Gumroad, LemonSqueezy, CodeCanyon

### Courses
Package your knowledge.

- Mini-course: $49-97
- Full course: $197-497
- Cohort-based: $997+

**Platforms**: Teachable, Podia, Maven

### Pros
- Passive income
- High margins (90%+)
- Scales infinitely

### Cons
- Upfront work
- Marketing required
- Support burden

### Real Numbers
- 100 sales × $49 = $4,900
- With 5 products: $10K+ achievable

---

## Strategy 3: SaaS (Software as a Service)

**The holy grail. Also the hardest.**

### The Formula
1. Find a painful problem
2. Build the simplest solution
3. Charge monthly
4. Iterate based on feedback
5. Scale marketing

### Pricing
- Free tier: Get users
- Starter: $29-49/mo
- Pro: $79-199/mo
- Enterprise: Custom

### Tech Stack (2026)
- Frontend: Next.js + Tailwind
- Backend: Serverless (Vercel/Cloudflare)
- Database: Supabase/Neon
- Auth: NextAuth/Clerk
- Payments: Stripe

### Pros
- Recurring revenue
- High valuation
- Can become passive

### Cons
- High upfront investment
- Long time to revenue
- Support burden
- Churn is constant

### Real Numbers
- 200 users × $49/mo = $9,800/mo
- Need ~50 free trials to get 1 paying customer

---

## Strategy 4: Content + Community

**Slow burn. Compounding returns.**

### The Flywheel
1. Create valuable content
2. Build an audience
3. Launch products to audience
4. Audience grows
5. Repeat

### Content Types
- Blog posts (SEO)
- Twitter threads (virality)
- YouTube (long-form)
- Newsletter (direct relationship)

### Monetization
- Sponsorships: $500-5,000/post
- Affiliate: 20-50% commission
- Products: Launch to audience
- Community: $9-49/mo

### Pros
- Compounding growth
- Multiple revenue streams
- Authority building

### Cons
- Slow (6-12 months)
- Requires consistency
- No guaranteed income
