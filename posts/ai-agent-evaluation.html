AI Agent QA & Evaluation: How to Score Your Agent's Performance

Testing is just half the battle. Evaluation is where you actually prove your agent works.

The 3 Pillars of AI Agent Evaluation

  • Automated Scorers: Use an LLM to grade another LLM. Define rubrics for correctness, tone, and efficiency.
  • Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Validation: Sample 5% of production turns for manual review to seed your golden dataset.
  • Regression Testing: Maintain a 'Golden Dataset' of complex agent conversations. Run it daily to ensure zero performance drift.
